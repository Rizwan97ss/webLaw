## Introduction

Your first consultation is an important step toward understanding your legal options.

## Bring Relevant Documents

Prepare contracts, correspondence, court papers, reports, or any documents connected to your matter.

## Write Down Questions

Before the meeting, write a list of questions so you do not forget anything important.
